Overview

Purpose

Hello! I am here to help you learn how to white water raft and help you have a good time on the Snake River. Here we offer three different packages to make sure you are getting the most of your rafting experience. The tours we take you on will pump you with a lot of adrenaline as we go through multiple class 3 rapids and even a class 4 rapid.

Audience

Who are the target Audience: Specifically we are looking for college students who want a quick vacation and a fun filled weekend.
What do they Want: To Have a break from classes and be able to rest their minds for a short time. Forgetting the stresses of the world.
What are their needs that aren't being met : They are not getting enough adrenaline in their daily lives.
